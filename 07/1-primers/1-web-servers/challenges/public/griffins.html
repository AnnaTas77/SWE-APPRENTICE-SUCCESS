Griffins

Griffins are mythical creatures with the body of a lion and the head, wings, and talons of an eagle. They are powerful and majestic beasts.

Griffins are often depicted as having a regal and fierce appearance. Their eagle head and wings are used for flight and their lion body and powerful legs are used for hunting and fighting. Griffins are also known for their keen eyesight and sharp talons, which make them formidable opponents.
