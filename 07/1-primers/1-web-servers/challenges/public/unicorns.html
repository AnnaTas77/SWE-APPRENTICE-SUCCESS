Unicorns

Unicorns are legendary creatures with a single, spiraled horn on their forehead. They are often associated with purity, magic, and grace.

Unicorns are typically depicted as having the body of a horse, but with a magical horn on their forehead. They are known for their speed and agility, and are said to have the ability to heal the sick and purify water. Unicorns are also often associated with rainbows and are said to be able to create them with a flick of their tail.
