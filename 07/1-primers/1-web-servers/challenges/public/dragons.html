Dragons

Dragons are large, winged, reptilian creatures that breathe fire. They are the rulers of the sky.

Dragons are often depicted as having a long neck, four legs, and a long tail. They have two large wings that are used for flight. They have a long, pointed snout with a row of sharp teeth. They have two small horns on their head. They have a long, muscular tail that is used for balance and to help them turn.
